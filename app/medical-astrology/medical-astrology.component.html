Medical astrology refers to the branch of medicine that relates the positions of planets and zodiac signs to body parts, body functions and diseases. It is a complex system of observation which is governed by a system of given rulerships. Modern medicine finds logic in the comparisons and assumptions that this ancient form of medicine is based on. The symbolisms used provide direct or indirect reference to the underlying diseases and their cures.
