OUR MISSION

At Heka, We ensure Homecare is more Personalized and Premium, by leveraging technology for better patient outcomes.

OUR VISION

To be compassionate service provider, caring for people and advancing health and wellbeing.

By integrating technology, artificial intelligence and deep learning algorithms for better health-care delivery and push preventive over curative healthcare, we strive to make our contributions more noteworthy.

We are building a connected care ecosystem, using IoT enabled devices, which helps with chronic disease management and emergency care while ensuring routine care by hospitals and clinicians, at the touch of a button.

At Heka Healthy You, We help our patrons lead healthier lives in their homes.

If you or a loved one is recovering from an illness, looking for transitioning from a hospital to home, or in need of specialized treatment, our knowledgeable and skilled staff are here to continue your care.

OUR FOCUS IS ON HELPING YOU TO GET WELL & REGAIN

Home healthcare is becoming a significant element in the value chain of healthcare services.

With the growing concerns around the quality of institutionalized care, family caregivers are demanding worthwhile solutions to provide care for their loved ones.

Higher levels of perceived stress, social isolation, lack of work-life balance, and negative impact on the emotional well-being of the family caregivers are some of the factors that are contributing to the growing demand for home care.

We provide a comprehensive continuum of care with specialized Doctors, Physiotherapists, Nursing specialists, Diagnostics services, Medicines, and trained attendants for your unique medical needs, all within the comfort of your home.
