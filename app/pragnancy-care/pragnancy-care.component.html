The journey from conception to childbirth and beyond is a beautiful yet intimidating one for most women. The lack of proper care in prenatal and postpartum stages can lead to severe complications.Heka Healthy You aids you in this journey, right from the planning phase to the delivery. We specialise in assisting our clients in high-risk pregnancies, neonatal care. Our trained experts will help you in all stages of the journey.

OUR PLANS

Parental Care

Getting regular Prenatal Care,ensures that you and baby are healthy, as expected. Regular care, support on nutrition, fitness and emotional well being helps to catch potential concerns early and reduces any further health & birth complications.

Post Pregnancy

Effective health care for a new mother is an important and continuing process, that begins right immediately after the delivery and extends to months of postpartum care. They are tailored to the needs of the mother and baby.

Continuum care

Holding a baby is euphoric for new parents. A Mother undergoes drastic change, mentally and physically during pregnancy and childbirth. Our plans supports rebuilding and regaining the strength. Emotional wellness, lactation issues etc are few concerns that any new mother seeks answers to.
