ROAD SAFETY

WHO estimates that 1.3 million people die, 20 – 50 million gets injured in road accidents globally every year. India records 80,000 deaths annually in road crashes, there is a road accident every minute, whereas one fatal accident takes lives every four minutes.

Ministry of Road Transport & Highways attributes 2 reasons for the accidents:

1. Carelessness of the driver

2. Lack of road safety awareness methods and techniques.

With the number of vehicles on our roads increasing with every passing hour, it’s of vital importance for everyone to have traffic awareness and understand and respect all the road safety rules in India.

Since road accidents involves roads, motor vehicles and human beings, hence it needs overall development of the strategies and interventions at multi - functional levels.

REASONS THAT HAMPER ROAD SAFETY ISSUES

Road safety in India is very poor. Despite several government rules and regulations, there are mishaps and accidents. Here are some of the issues:

CONSEQUENCES OF ROAD ACCIDENTS

  • Around 400 persons are dying every day in road accident deaths in India
  • Young males under the age group of 25 years are dying more than young females.
  • Kids and youngsters (14 to 29 years age) are dying more in road crashes.
  • Loss of Bread earning member to a family & household productivity loss.
  • Huge financial loss in road crash victims emergency and medical treatment.
  • Workplace productivity loss due to non-reporting of employee.
  • Loss to business because of untimely dispatch.
  • Loss to insurance companies.
  • Traffic Congestion & inconvenience.
