Life Support & Safety is the care that first-responders, healthcare professionals or trained professional impart upon the anyone suffering from cardiac arrest, obstructed airways or respiratory distress as a first aid. Nearly 45% of cardiac arrest victims have a chance of surviving with a CPR trained bystander around. Heka Healthy You believes that in order to make our society aware and prepared for such commonly occurring yet life threatening ailments, it is imperative to train individuals in Life Support & Safety. Therefore, we offer hands-on training by certified professionals in Emergency care, First aid, Road safety protocols & Safe electrical training.
