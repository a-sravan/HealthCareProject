INTEGRATED CARE MANAGEMENT

COVID-19 has brought out a startling reminder that the healthcare system needs to adopt healthcare automation toward integrated care management.

At Heka Healthy You, we are working to find solutions to promote preventive care through our connected health solutions and digital transformation, therefore finding ways to manage and address patient expectations.

Through the convergence of eHealth and smart and connected health care, we help our patrons to

  • Implement new modes of care delivery
  • Electronic health systems for better care management
  • Efficient disease monitoring
  • Medical records management
  • Real -Time health monitoring
  • Modern Treatment options
  • Virtual & Remote Patient Monitoring
  • Explore avenues for continuity of care
  • Compare treatment outcomes
  • Manage medication requirements
  • Reduce patient readmission rates
  • Increase patient satisfaction
  • Chronic care management
  • Create platforms for patient empowerment
  • Tools for early detection and disease prevention

We work on patient-centric care and patient-participative models, to make healthcare management accessible and delivering excellence through our innovative solutions.
