Any operation is incomplete without comprehensive aftercare. While the operation itself may last a small duration, operative aftercare can range anywhere from a few days to months at end. A well-planned routine will ensure that the patient does not contract any post-operative pulmonary complication (PCCs) or infections. To ensure a safe and speedy recovery, one should be aided by well-trained and professional staff that understands the medical complications that may arise during the post-op phase. Heka Healthy You has a range of trained professionals that will assist you through this journey and make your recovery a pleasant one!
