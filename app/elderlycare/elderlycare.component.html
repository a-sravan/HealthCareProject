According to WHO, “Health in old age shouldn’t defined by the absence of disease.” Staying healthy pertains to remaining both mentally and physically fit. Aged care aims to provide the comfort and assistance that the elderly citizens deserve in growing old with dignity. Combined with the effect of urbanisation on joint families, it becomes apparent that not every elderly person would be able to be supported and monitored by a loved one at all times. To bridge this gap, Heka HealthyYou provides our customers with Elderly Care services, that will care for the elderly like our own.

Care At Home

Recovery Program
